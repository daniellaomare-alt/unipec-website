About Our School

Learn more about UNIPEC Baby Lounge

Our Mission

To provide a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where every child can develop academically, socially, and emotionally. We are committed to fostering a love for learning that will last a lifetime.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution in Otuo and Edo State, recognized for excellence in early childhood and primary education, producing confident, creative, and compassionate young learners.

What Makes Us Special

Our experienced teachers, child-centered approach, modern facilities, and strong community values create an environment where children thrive. We blend traditional Nigerian values with contemporary educational methods.

Our Learning Approach

We use a holistic, child-centered approach that combines the Nigerian National Curriculum with international best practices. Our methods include hands-on learning, interactive lessons, and personalized attention to ensure every child reaches their full potential.
